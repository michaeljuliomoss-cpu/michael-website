About Me

Get to know the person behind the pixels — your neighbor in Nassau.

Hey, I'm Michael 👋

I'm a web designer and digital services provider based right here in Nassau, Bahamas. For the past few years, I've been helping small businesses — from hair salons on East Bay Street to cafés at Cable Beach — build an online presence that actually works.

My journey started when I built a simple website for a friend's business. It was basic, but it got them results within the first month. That experience showed me something powerful: even a straightforward website can transform a small business. That's when I knew this was what I wanted to do.

Today, I've worked with businesses across Nassau and the wider Bahamas. What makes me different? I'm personal, I'm local, and I genuinely care about your success. You're not a ticket number — you're my neighbor. I answer my own phone, I handle my own emails, and I'm there when you need me.

Now I'm expanding beyond just websites. I build AI tools, automation systems, and digital solutions that help Nassau businesses work smarter. From AI chatbots that handle customer inquiries 24/7 to automated marketing funnels — I'm building the tech stack that helps small businesses compete.
